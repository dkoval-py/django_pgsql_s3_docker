Learn Programming with Drones

The course aims to provide the students with an overview of the role of drones play in modern society, developing their flight applications along the way. Through the building of multiple coding projects, each of which introducing new concepts with drones, students will learn to expand their perspective and gain a strong foundations of problem analysis and solving skills.

Details
  • The course is composed of 10 classes in form of 5 meetings.
  • The meeting is on weekend or afterschool which lasts 90 minutes.
  • Each group of 2 students will receive their own drone during the course.
  • We provide safety googles and three additional batteries with charger.
Requirements
  • Students age is 10-15 years old.
  • Participants should have a Android tablet or laptop with Windows system.
Overview

After learning basics of Blockly language, the student will be able to program the mini-drones in block-based environment. He will not only learn to remote control drones, but engage in programming them for autonomous missions. The participants will compete in air races and perform indoor drone swarms. Aviation rules and saftey will be covered in the program as well.

Summary

During the classes, participants will learn about

  • Science: physics, aerodynamics and classical mechanics of drones.
  • Technology: drone applications for solving real world problems.
  • Engineering: software, electrical and systems engineering of drones.
  • Math: mathematical solutions for deploying drone techonlogies.
  • Is the drone safe?

    • We are using mini-drones designed for education of kids. They weigh only 70 grams and have a size of hand. The drones automatically cut off power for saftey of kids in case of touching. As well, we provide safety googles for children for eye protection.
