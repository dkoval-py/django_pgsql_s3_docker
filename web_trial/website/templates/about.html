RoboCool
Vision

RoboCool creates a new generation of Roboticists through a series of programs and technologies, designed especially for children. We empower our students with 21-century skills to train the next generation of innovators.

Educational Robotics

tudents learn the fundamentals of robotics through programming up-to-date robotic technologies

Problem Solving

Children deploy latest robotic platforms to realize their own solutions for complex problems

STEM

Participants engage in hands-on activities with robots to learn the associated STEM concepts

Our Team

Provides creative learning environment

RoboCool is founded by a Robotics engineer who has a double degree from top institutions in Europe. Farag combines his experience of Robotics field with educational background and familiarity with the latest STEM trends to create the perfect atmosphere of learning. He believes extracurricular is the perfect way to share a new paradigm for a world-class education. Our programs utilize recent robotic technologies as a gateway to cultivate students' innovation and critical thinking. Therefore, we invite all school students to participate in a full week learning journey about the robots' world.
